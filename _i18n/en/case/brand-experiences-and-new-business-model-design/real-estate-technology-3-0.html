Real Estate Technology 3.0

Project｜Real Estate Technology 3.0
Category｜Brand Experiences and New Business Model Design
Clients｜Yung Ching Realty Group
Year｜2012 - 2014

Since 2012, Yung-Ching Realty Group has been working with our team in hopes of developing a new generation real estate consultancy service model to enhance service quality and efficiency. During the R&D process of real estate service innovation, we helped to spark new markets and technology ideas by various theme-based interviews with various types of customers, inviting front-line brokers to discuss service gaps, and by collecting cross-cutting benchmark cases and technological trends. We not only exported “precision matching” that met the needs of consumers, we were able to produce the follow-up “Real Estate Technology 3.0” innovation service model.

Different from the past where a lot of printed information needed to be carried around and the lack of information due to diverse customer needs, Real Estate Technology 3.0 has integrated technology tools and cloud systems so that brokers can serve customers anytime and anywhere through tablets and smart phones, increasing customer interaction. They are able to explore customer needs and help accurately find property that suits customers, which not only improves efficiency, it also helps customers save 20% of time surveying houses.

“Real Estate Technology 3.0” integrates the current trend of “All Access Service” where consumers can search for objects, receive information, and have interaction through stores, websites, smart phones or tablets. Their actions are also recorded so that Yung Ching’s virtual and physical services are fully connected, breaking the restrictions of space and geography.
