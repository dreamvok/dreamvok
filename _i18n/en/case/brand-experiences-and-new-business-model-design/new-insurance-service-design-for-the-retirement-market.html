New Insurance Service Design for the Retirement Market

Project｜New Insurance Service Design for the Retirement Market
Category｜ Brand Experiences and New Business Model Design
Clients｜Prudential International Life Insurance
Year｜2016 - 2017

By 2018, Taiwan’s population over the age of 65 will exceed 14% and it will become an aging society. By 2025, the population over the age of 65 will be as high as 20%, and Taiwan will officially become a hyper-aged society. Are we ready to face our lives of retirement? In the past, people’s needs and plans financially were mostly preparing for disability, dementia and loss of life. However, as life expectancy continues to grow, this traditional approach to financial planning is no longer effective.

Therefore, we worked with Prudential Life Insurance to collect official statistics from the Directorate General of Budget, Accounting and Statistics, foreign trend reports and customer segmentation experience analysis, and discovered that retirees are hopeful and confident about their retirement life not only because of abstract figures, they have actual plans for their future lives and have the ability to achieve them.

第二人生設計工具包

Therefore, after 10 months, we determined the “9 lifestyles” of retirement life for the 50+ group in Taiwan and we also developed the “Retirement Life Design Toolkit.” Through this analysis tool, 50 groups can become designers of their future lives and plan their lives systematically using the same methods as designing iPhones. This also is related to Prudential Life Insurance’s new approaches of customer service, system construction planning and education training, and extends to create innovative services and products that are more in line with the needs of the future retirement market.
