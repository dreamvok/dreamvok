Innovative Application of Coating Technology

Project｜Innovative Application of Coating Technology
Category｜Patent Technology Commercialization and R&D Distribution Consultant
Clients｜ITRI Material and Chemical Research Laboratories
Year｜2016

The ITRI Material and Chemical Research Laboratories had a stain-resistant and easy to clean coating technology which could prevent the surface of items from getting wet or fog up. However, there are many similar sprays or materials available for lower prices in the automotive beauty industry and consumers were unable to recognize the true value of this coating technology. The technical team tried to come up with new positioning and new value for the technology’s future.

A set of “technology value exploration tools” was developed for this case study to analyze the coating technology together with the R & D team. On the other hand, the project also focused on trends in user lifestyles, and finally developed application scenarios that included transportation tool tube cleaning, exterior wall dirt prevention, and indoor planting energy control. Through joint research with biotech R & D organizations, we successfully planned and organized R&D strategies for the next 3-5 years. Besides highlighting the “technical value” and “potential cross-domain applications” of the coating, we also promoted business cooperation with downstream and inter-industry partners.
