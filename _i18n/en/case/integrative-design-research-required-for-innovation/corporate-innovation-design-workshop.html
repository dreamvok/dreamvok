Corporate Innovation Design Workshop

Project｜Corporate Innovation Design Workshop
Category｜Integrative design research required for innovation
Clients｜Pfizer and other corporate clients
Year｜2012 - Present

Faced with rapidly changing markets, enterprises have begun to attach importance to internal innovation and change. They not only have to adjust working methods within the organization, but also must have the ability to deal with more difficult challenges arising during innovation and transformation. Through a systematic approach, DreamVok helps companies learn from the perspective of customers and future trends to "redefine" their products and services.

We use “service design” and “lean start-up”methods in our workshops, and unlike business processes in the past, we also include voices of customers and stakeholders. We invite them to speak so businesses are able to come face to face with real customers and listen to their needs, further creating win-win solutions in the workshops. As a guide for businesses to promote innovation and design, DreamVok’s design thinking training not only enhances innovative energy, but also helps gather consensus in the organization to lay the foundation for strategy implementation.
