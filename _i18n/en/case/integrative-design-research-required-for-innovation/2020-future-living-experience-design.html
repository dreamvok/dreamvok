2020 Future Living Experience Design

Project｜2020 Future Living Experience Design
Category｜Integrative design research required for innovation
Clients｜ITRI Commercialization and Industry Service Center
Year｜2014

With the changes in time and demand, people’s lifestyles and behavioral preferences also change, which in turn affects market direction and drives economic trends. In view of this, DreamVok and the ITRI Dechnology team conducted the 2020 life-style trend analysis and user behavior research, in hopes of helping Taiwanese enterprises accurately predict and analyze future opportunities for innovation.

趨勢分析和創新方向的關鍵報告

We focused on four future life scenarios: “Living,” “Travel,” “Learning,” and “Health Care.” We presented key reports on trend analysis and the direction of innovation. Finally, we explored the reasons for unmet needs in real life or people's expectations of the future.

未來生活情境發展藍圖

Based on second-hand information research, we analyzed domestic and international cases and business models in the fields of technology and design and proposed trend forecasts. At the same time, we worked together with multidisciplinary experts during co-creation design workshops to discuss various issues including future entry points, overall persona, needs and innovation opportunities, and presented the development roadmap of four types of life scenarios through visual images.
